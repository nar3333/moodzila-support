# Moodzila Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 1, 2025**

## Introduction

Moodzila ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mood tracking application.

## Data Collection and Storage

**Local Storage Only**
- All mood entries, notes, and personal data are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
- We do not collect, transmit, store, or access any of your personal data on external servers
- Your mood tracking data never leaves your device

**No User Accounts**
- Moodzila does not require user accounts, registration, or login
- We do not collect email addresses, names, or any personal identification information

## Third-Party Services

**Google AdMob**
- Moodzila uses Google AdMob to display advertisements
- AdMob may collect certain device information for ad serving and analytics
- This may include: device identifiers, IP address, and general location
- AdMob operates under Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings

**Apple Notifications**
- If you enable daily reminders, notification permissions are handled by iOS
- Notifications are scheduled locally on your device
- We do not track or collect notification data

## Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:
- Personal identification information
- Mood entries or journal notes
- Usage analytics or behavior tracking
- Location data (beyond what AdMob may collect for regional ads)
- Contact lists or social connections
- Photos or media files

## Children's Privacy

Moodzila does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for general audiences. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information to us, please contact us.

## Data Security

Your data security is important to us:
- All data remains on your device
- We use Apple's secure SwiftData framework
- No cloud synchronization or backup to external servers
- Data is protected by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, etc.)

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- You have complete control over your data
- Delete the app to permanently remove all data
- No data remains on external servers after app deletion
- You can disable notifications at any time in Settings

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of Moodzila after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
